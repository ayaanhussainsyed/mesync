How it works

Three ingredients. One mirror of you.

1
Take the onboarding
A short quiz scores your Big Five personality traits. The twin starts with a baseline of who you are — not a blank LLM.
2
Journal & connect your apps
Write entries or record voice notes. Plug in Spotify, GitHub, Gmail, WhatsApp — MeSync turns everything into embedded memory.
3
Chat across personas
Talk to your future self over letters. Stress-test a decision with a devil's advocate. Vent to the angry you. It's still you, just through different masks.
Features

Everything a mirror of you should do.

Chat with eight personas
Default, Future Self (letters), Professional, Angry, Sad, Emo, Hype, Chill, Devil's Advocate. Pick one per thread.
Daily journal (text + voice)
An open-book UI with a compose page and an archive page. Voice notes transcribed, moods tagged, everything fed back into memory.
Letters from later
Every few days your future self drops a letter in your inbox — unprompted, in letter form, about something real from your life.
Memory graph
The people, places, feelings, and goals in your words — extracted as a navigable semantic graph. Dragable, zoomable, yours.
Drift meter
Mood distribution, writing cadence, trait shifts. See how you've moved over time — not what you feel, what's actually changed.
Decision simulator
Describe what you're torn between. Your twin branches three futures — 3 months, 1 year, 3 years — rooted in what it knows about you.
